How Does a Completed Dashboard Look?

Dashboard

Finalize the layout by adding and placing Images  that represent the entities and process(es)  involved in the business process that the report is based off of. Usually this involves a  Transaction, a Location, People, and a Service/Product.  In our case, three dimension tables were involved (Groups, Members, Plans) as well as one Fact table (Memberships). Be sure to also add an Image of the company logo and  a text box to display the company name. Finally, add a color theme to the report visualizations. Here the ‘Electric’ color theme was implemented to match the colors of the company logo. 




Power BI Dashboards should be designed to tell a story through visualizations and contain only the most important elements according to the BRD
